About our in house Mixology

Boasting some of the very best mixologists, No.8 Mill Street really is the ultimate destination for the cocktail conisseur in us all. An impressive cocktail menu is the jewel in this unique destination in the heart of Westport, Co Mayo. Packed to the brim with everything from the classics to one off craft cocktails! In fact we do so many types of Cocktails We Dont Even Bother With Classics On Our Menu Anymore, Just Tell Us What You Want And We Will Make It for you! We Hope To See You Soon For Your Very Own Custom Cocktail Night! No.8 Mill Street also offers a wide selection of ‘normal’ drinks. With everything from worldly Spririts, craft beers, ciders and soft drinks, you certainly won’t go thirsty.

What We Do and Why We Love It

"No 8 Mill Street is a local business born of our love for Spanish food and culture. Having lived and spent many holidays in Spain and great times enjoying tapas bars, we were inspired to bring an authentic experience to Westport to share what we think is the authentic family way of eating. Our concept is to serve small dishes tapas style….. it is all about the sharing and enjoyment of eating and tasting some amazing flavours. Our aim is to make every customer feel at home with its informal buzzy atmosphere and welcoming staff. No 8 combines best quality food wines and beers. authentic preparation, friendly service and great prices delivered in a contemporary space with a neighbourhood vibe." -Jim and Ruth (Owners)

About Our Wines

As well as boasting a fully stocked bar serving beers and spirits we stock a vast selection of fine wines from all around the world. The wines we have carefully selected compliment the food we serve and are not avilable to purchase in high street supermarkets. We stock over 500 bottles of wine at any given time across 50 different varieties from Spain, France, Italy, Portugal, California, South Africa, Chile and Argentina. We host a number of wine tasting events throughout the year which have proven a big success as we serve some of our amazing tapas dishes to compliment the bouquet. Make sure to follow us on Facebook and Instagram to find out about our events. Our intimate surroundings, tasty food and fine wine offer the perfect venue for that special occasion or even simply an evening out.
